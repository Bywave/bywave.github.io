Visibility

Visibility of content in different devices. It uses media queries to show and hide a content in appropriate devices.



Show and Hide

An options to make the content to show/hide on all devices, use a class name ".hide" and "show".

HTML example markup
<div class="show"></div>
<div class="hide"></div>


Reponsive Visibility

If you want to Hide some content in different devices. Use these classes "hide-on-small", "hide-on-medium", "hide-on-default", "hide-on-large". Resize your browser to see it in action.

Hide me on Small Screen (Mobile).
Hide me on Medium Screen (Tablet).
Hide me on Default Screen (Desktop).
Hide me on Large Screen (Desktop Wide Screen).

HTML example markup
<div class="hide-on-small">...</div>
<div class="hide-on-medium">...</div>
<div class="hide-on-default">...</div>
<div class="hide-on-large">...</div>


Like the hide you can also Show a content in different devices. Use these classes "show-on-small", "show-on-medium", "show-on-default", "show-on-large".

Show me on Small Screen (Mobile).
Show me on Medium Screen (Tablet).
Show me on Default Screen (Desktop).
Show me on Large Screen (Desktop Wide Screen).

HTML example markup
<div class="show-on-small">...</div>
<div class="show-on-medium">...</div>
<div class="show-on-default">...</div>
<div class="show-on-large">...</div>
